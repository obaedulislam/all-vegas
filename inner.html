This luxury hotel and casino on the Las Vegas Strip features celebrity-owned restaurants, The Forum Shops, an upscale spa and several swimming pools. McCarran International Airport is less than 6 kilometers away. Dining options include a Pub & Grill restaurant, and several restaurants with famous chefs leading them. The famous Bacchanal Buffet and casual dining choices like Beijing Noodle No. 9 are located on site. All guest rooms offer home appliances and modern TV. Hotel rooms include an en-suite bathroom with toiletries. During these unprecedented times we are committed to the health and safety of our guests and employees and have implemented general protocols when visiting out family of resorts.
