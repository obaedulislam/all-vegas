AMAZING MAGIC WITH TOMMY WIND

Tommy Wind, like many children, was introduced to magic by his grandfather. Unlike other kids, Wind, who is now an adult, pursued his magical inspiration to star in his own show on the Las Vegas Strip.

Amazing Magic with Tommy Wind brings his talents to the Alexis Park All Suite Resort. Wind's show combines his two passion - magic and music - into illusions including a levitating guitar, a woman who disappears while playing a harp and a dueling drum sequence. The show also features comedy routines, beautiful dancers and more.

Wind has performed in many of his own original shows, combining illusion with music, in New York City and Atlantic City. He has also appeared in TV shows including Criss Angel's "Mindfreak" and NBC's "I Do!" Wind has also won numerous awards, including being acknowledged by the International Brotherhood of Magicians.
